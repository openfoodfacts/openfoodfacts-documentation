## Understanding and being compliant with our data licences

<Callout type="warn">
**Legal Compliance Required**

Understanding and complying with our data licenses is mandatory for all users of Open Food Facts data.

</Callout>

**Image Rights Notice**

Product images may contain graphical elements subject to copyright or other rights that may, in some cases, be reproduced (quotation rights or fair use).

</Callout>
